About the AU

Everything Has Changed is an HP AU that focuses mainly on the hypothesis that Hermione Granger is secretly Sirius Black and Marlene McKinnon's daughter. EHC is a multi-ship AU, although in most interactions is Fred x Hermione. The main changes from canon are that Hermione is a year older, Fred and George are one year younger and Ginny and Ron are twins, and Hermione's childhood - which is never explicitly described in canon. From the beginning of the 1st year the story progresses very differently.

About the AU

What if Draco and Hermione became friends from early on? And what if from that friendship something else blossomed? This AU starts diverging from the HP cannon when Draco and Hermione strike a friendship in 2nd year, and of course, Draco's opinions on blood status are changed. In about 5th/6th year, they enter a relationship with eachother. In the end, Draco deflects his family's legacy and joins Hermione's independent fight against Voldemort.

Sub-AU: Back to Glory

In my Dramione AU, sometimes I half-way relate it to the "Everything Has Changed" AU, in the way Hermione is also a secret Black, but this time she's not Sirius' daughter but Regulus' and Bartemius Jr. Crouch's sister's, Anna Crouch's (OC), daughter. Her real name is Astra Walburga Black, Astra meaning star, fitting in the Black name convention, and Walburga because it's Regulus' mother's name. When she and Draco get married they, together, join the Ancient and Noble Houses of Black and Malfoy, bring them to their former glory, but of course with better morals this time around. They name their kids according to pureblood traditions (the first children have their parents or grandparents names as their middle names), and then the Black House name convention, where the names are the name of stars and constellations in latin. In this AU, Narcissa (Draco's mother) is, although officially a Black, actually the secret daughter of Belladona Black neè. Paulvier and Thoros Nott.
